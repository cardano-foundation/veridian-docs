# Login and Vote Receipts

To check vote receipts in Cardano Ballot, you must login using your wallet.

#### Login

Once the first vote is cast, a popup will appear asking for login to check the receipt.
Alternatively, the wallet button in the top left corner of the Cardano Ballot window can be clicked to expand the drop-down menu, and the "Login" option can be selected.

In the Identity Wallet, a pop up will appear prompting you to sign the login information using your identifier.
Click on the “Sign” button to confirm your identity.

#### Vote Receipts

Now that you are logged in Cardano Ballot, you can check your vote receipt by clicking on the “View Receipt” button.
