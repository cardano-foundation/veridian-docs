# Support

Please refer to [this page](../sandbox) if you'd like to contact us in relation to a sandbox.

For all other requests, we offer the following support streams to fit your needs:
- Submit a [support ticket](https://cardanofoundation.atlassian.net/servicedesk/customer/portal/14)
- Submit a [feature request](https://github.com/cardano-foundation/veridian-wallet/issues/new?assignees=&labels=&projects=&template=feature_request.yml&title=%5BFEATURE%5D+), [bug report](https://github.com/cardano-foundation/veridian-wallet/issues/new?assignees=&labels=&projects=&template=bug_report.yml&title=%5BBUG%5D+), or [discussion](https://github.com/cardano-foundation/veridian-wallet/discussions) directly in our Github repository
- Reach out on our [Discord](https://discord.com/invite/9yNneV8Ktv)

We encourage the use of Github and Discord in the spirit of open source!
