# Welcome

This guide provides a comprehensive overview of the Veridian Wallet and its core functionalities.
Here, you’ll find clear explanations of the app’s features, along with step-by-step tutorials and videos to help you navigate and use the wallet with confidence.

The Veridian Wallet is the world’s first KERI-based mobile wallet, offering a secure, decentralized way to manage your digital identity.
Whether you’re setting up your wallet for the first time, or exploring advanced features like group multi-signature flows, this guide will walk you through the process with accessible, easy-to-follow instructions.

Let's get started!
